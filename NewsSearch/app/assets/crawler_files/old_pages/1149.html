Use your Google Wallet Card to pay with your Google Wallet Balance in-store at millions of Debit MasterCard® locations. You can also use it to withdraw cash at an ATM, and you'll get instant notifications for every transaction, right on your phone.

Send money.
Spend instantly.

Send money to anyone in the US with a Gmail address, or request money on-the-go. It's easy, fast, and free to send money directly from your bank account, debit card or Wallet Balance.
Received money? Spend it instantly in stores or withdraw cash from ATMs with the Google Wallet Card. You can also transfer it directly to your bank account.
