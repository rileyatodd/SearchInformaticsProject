An auto dealer conglomerate, a pipeline company and the nation's largest food services firm topped the list of Houston's Fortune 500 companies based  on stock performance so far this year.

Group 1 Automotive, Kinder Morgan and Sysco Corp. were among eight Fortune 500 companies that outperformed the Dow Jones Industrial Average's gain of 5.8 percent in 2014 through Dec. 10. The broader market S&P 500 index gained 9.6 percent during the period.

The roster of Houston's Fortune 500 companies is heavily tied to the energy industry, and many stocks have taken a hit in 2014.

In the images above, take a look at the companies stock performance as of mid-morning trading Thursday, Dec. 11. Crude oil closed at $60.94 Wednesday, down 38 percent for the year and a five-year low.

The 52-week high and low is also shown for the period, along with the trailing 12-month earnings per share (EPS) provided by Bloomberg.
