Pure x Hennessy x Strivers Row

Posted on December 13, 2014 | By Maribel Molina
  • Print
  • —

Houstons hottest rappers came together for the opening of Pure x Strivers Row at the Galleria shopping mall on Dec. 11. Paul Wall, Bun B and Slim Thug made for a star-studded event with music from DJ Mr. Rogers and complimentary Hennessy VS drinks.
