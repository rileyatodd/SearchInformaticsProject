Bill Cosby Still Won't Apologize! Thinks Only 'Black Media' Will Remain Neutral In Reports About Him — Is That The Case?

12/15/2014 1:28 PM ET | Filed under: Sad Sad • Crazzzzy • News • Bill Cosby

Bill Cosby has stayed relatively silent as the rape accusations against him have been piling up.

We previously mentioned, the media FINALLY got through to him, even if only for a brief moment.

He wouldn't comment on all the rape and sex abuse allegations but he did make one thing clear:

"Let me say this. I only expect the black media to uphold the standards of excellence in journalism and when you do that you have to go in with a neutral mind."

We're pretty sure, regardless of a news outlet's demographic, most are posting both his and his victims' claims without bias.

[ Related: Sherri Shepherd Twitter Rant On The Bill Cosby Rape Scandal! ]

Even Bossip — a predominately black news website — only reposted another site's article about Cosby's "neutral mind" accusation and simply added:

"Do you [think] there's a racial bias with the reporting of Bill Cosby's recent rape accusations??"

Looks like regardless of race media is pretty much agreed that things look bad for Cosby and they're acknowledging that everyone has their own opinion.

Cosby is innocent until he's proven guilty in a court of law, but also the mounting allegations against him make it hard to stay unbiased, so we're sure he's upset at how bad he's being made to look.

But that's just how it is. Things DO look bad because they ARE bad!

He apparently ended his brief phone call interview saying:

"…they don't want me talking to the media."

Yeah, we kinda have an idea why…

Maybe remaining silent (or confessing to EVERYTHING) is his best bet for now.
