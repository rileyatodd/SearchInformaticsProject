The Goodfellows tradition of providing holiday toys to needy children began 100 years ago.

Last year, Houston's generosity enabled Goodfellows to provide toys to nearly 50,000 children in almost 10,000 families.  Please take a moment to read more about the families we have helped and donate today to help keep the tradition alive.

 

Family pulls together after shooting
By Mayra Cruz
Sunday, December 14, 2014

Stephanie Mitchell's days are hectic, beginning at the crack of dawn. She wakes up her three children at 5 a.m. to get them ready for school before 7 a.m.

By the time she's done dropping off her two sons and daughter, she sets out to remind her husband, Emory Mitchell, 32, to take his medications before shuttling him to doctors' appointments around town in the Medical Center and Spring Branch area.

Been through a lot

Emory Mitchell suffered a spinal cord injury after being attacked in a shooting incident while working as a security guard at a bar one night in 2010. He has needed a wheelchair since then.
