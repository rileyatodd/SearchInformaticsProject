Welcome to Hearst Newspapers Career Site

The Hearst Corporation is one of the largest and most diversified media companies in our nation. At over 120 years old, the privately held Hearst Corporation employs more than 20,000 individuals in its varied areas of interest, including magazines, newspapers, television stations, cable networks, Internet businesses and more. The Hearst Newspapers division, with over 6,500 employees, publishes 13 daily newspapers with complementary Web sites across the United States. Among the daily papers and sites are standouts such as Houston Chronicle/chron.com, San Francisco Chronicle/sfgate.com, Albany Times Union/timesunion.com, San Antonio Express News/mysanantonio.com and Beaumont Enterprise/beaumontenterprise.com.

At Hearst Newspapers we believe that we are at the forefront of reinventing the news and information business. We see the changing landscape as ripe with opportunity. Our industry is being transformed into a digital world and we are looking for people who are eager to facilitate our innovative efforts. As our business model changes our drive to hire the best and brightest is paramount. Hearst Newspapers is committed to hiring and developing employees who are equipped to lead our business, along with our readers, advertisers and subscribers into the new possibilities.

Hearst Newspapers is an equal opportunity employer, dedicated to promoting a culturally diverse work environment.
