Home Delivery

How can I change the amount of days I receive my paper?

Please contact our Subscriber Services department at 713-362-7211 (Houston area) or outside of Houston area, customers should call 1-888-220-7211. Business hours are from 5:30am–7:00pm, Monday through Friday, 7:30am–11:30am on Saturday, Sunday.

How do I change my delivery address?

Please contact our Subscriber Services department at 713-362-7211 (Houston area) or outside of Houston area, customers should call 1-888-220-7211. Business hours are from 5:30am–7:00pm, Monday through Friday, 7:30am–11:30am on Saturday, Sunday.

How can I report a missed/wet/damaged paper for today?

For a previous day? Simply go to www.houstonchronicle.com/subscribers. Once you have logged in proceed to the top menu and move your mouse over "Subscriber Services", then click "Delivery Issue".

Can I tell the delivery person where to leave my paper?

Yes. Please contact our Subscriber Services department at 713-362-7211. If you live outside of the Houston area please call 1-888-220-7211. Business hours are from 5:30am–7:00pm, Monday through Friday, 7:30am–11:30am on Saturday, Sunday.

When will my delivery begin?

Delivery will begin on the next scheduled delivery day, which is determined by your frequency of delivery.

Can I still get the Houston Chronicle if I move out of the area?

Yes, there are 2 options to keep you in touch with our local news coverage. We offer a mail subscription, where we can mail the newspaper to you or you may sign up for our All Digital Access. The All Digital Access grants you access to our premium website, Houstonchronicle.com, iPad app and eNewspaper (exact replica of The Chronicle printed edition available online). Please visit us at www.houstonchronicle.com/subscribers to subscribe today.

How do I stop delivery while on vacation?

You may stop your delivery while on vacation by visiting www.houstonchronicle.com/subscribers. Once you have logged in, proceed to the top menu and move your mouse over "Subscriber Services", then click "Vacation Hold".

Can I donate my papers while on vacation to Newspapers In Education?

Since our vacation policy change in January 2012, this option is no longer available. However, If you are stopping your delivery for 21 days or less we can have the papers saved and delivered to you upon your return.

How do I cancel my subscription on the Subscriber Services site?

To request a cancellation on your subscription, please call the Chronicle Subscriber Services department at 713-362-7211 (Houston area) or outside of Houston area, customers should call 1-888-220-7211. Business hours are from 5:30am–7:00pm, Monday through Friday, 7:30am–11:30am on Saturday and Sunday.

Digital Access

How do I activate my All Digital Access?

Please go to www.houstonchronicle.com/activate, then simply follow the instructions to activate your account or upgrade your account to meet the minimum requirements for HoustonChronicle.com access.

What are the benefits to All Digital Access?

The All Digital Access includes unlimited access to HoustonChronicle.com and the iPad® app which reimagines the newspaper by providing interactive puzzles in addition to the daily sections, and the eNewspaper which provides the contents of the newspaper digitally but in a more traditional format, allowing you to flip through pages whether you’re in Houston or on vacation.

I attempted to log into my account and received a message the system does not recognize my login information. What can I do?

Before gaining access to HoustonChronicle.com, you will need to activate your account. If you currently receive home delivery of the Houston Chronicle, visit www.houstonchronicle.com/activate to confirm your account meets the minimum access requirements. If you qualify for access, you will be asked for the email address associated with your subscription. Select [VERIFY], then choose a password and select [FINISH].

Can I use my iPad® app user name and password to access premium content?

HoustonChronicle.com requires a separate activation process. Your iPad® app login is not tied to the login information for HoustonChronicle.com. Visit www.houstonchronicle.com/activate to set up and activate your account.

Can I read stories on my tablet or mobile phone?

Yes, because HoustonChronicle.com was created using responsive design technology, which automatically adapts the site content so it always reads perfectly on the digital device of your choice, you can enjoy the site just as you would from a desktop or laptop.

How do I access the eNewspaper?

Register your account at www.houstonchronicle.com/subscribers. Once registered proceed to login, place your mouse over eNewspaper located on the top menu bar, select "Access Today’s eNewspaper".

What should I do if I tried to activate my subscription and got a message that it cannot be found?

Typically this happens when the information does not match our database. To better assist you, please call the Chronicle Subscriber Services at 713-362-7211 (Houston area) or outside of Houston area, customers should call 1-888-220-7211. Business hours are from 5:30am–7:00pm, Monday through Friday, 7:30am–11:30am on Saturday and Sunday.

How can I change my password?

The instructions below will help you reset your password for your newspaper subscription account:

  1. Click the following link to begin the process to reset your password: https://myaccount.houstonchronicle.com/ForgotPassword.aspx.
  2. Enter the email address you used to sign up for your subscription and click Send Request. If you cannot remember the email address you used, please call Customer Service at 713-362-7211 or email us for assistance.
    1. An email will be sent to the email address you entered with a link to reset the password.
      1. Click on the link.
      2. Enter your new password (password is case sensitive, and must be at least 6 characters).
      3. Click submit, Once your password is changed or reset, your account is automatically updated. Please use your new password to log into houstonchronicle.com and to manage your account online.
