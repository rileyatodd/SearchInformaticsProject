Introduction
The Hockey News has been providing the most comprehensive coverage of the world of hockey since 1947. With over two million dedicated readers, The Hockey News is the authoritative source of hockey and the #1 hockey publication in North America.

The Hockey News gives you the inside scoop on hockey like no other publication. 
- Interviews, photos, stats and predictions. 
- Team stories from the best writers in all 30 NHL cities. 
- Coverage of minor pro, college and junior leagues. 
- Weekly features that take you inside the game.
