If you witness a news event, the ABC wants to hear from you. We would like you to send us your newsworthy photos, videos, audio clips or even written eyewitness accounts for consideration for use on ABC News.

Upload your media

Use the form below to submit news content to ABC News Online.

Please make sure you have read the Terms and Conditions before submitting.

There is a size limit of 20MB for files and they must be one of these formats:

Images:
  • GIF (.gif)
  • JPEG (.jpg)
Audio:
  • MP3 (.mp3)
  • Windows Media Audio (.wma)
Video:
  • MPEG4 (.mp4)
  • Quicktime Movie (.mov)
  • Microsoft Video (.avi)
  • Flash Video (.flv)
  • Mobile Video (.3gp)
  • Windows Video (.wmv)
