Sydney hostage drama: Scenes from the 16-hour standoff

900
Shares
Share
Tweet
Share
What's This?
By Dustin Drankoski2014-12-15 18:01:15 +0100

Police stormed a Sydney cafe on Tuesday morning local time to end a dramatic standoff that lasted more than 16 hours. A gunman held a group of people in the Lindt cafe in Martin Place, which is located in a very busy area of the city.

See also: Uber intros surge pricing during Sydney hostage siege, then backtracks after user outcry

Some hostages were able to escape, but the fate of others remains unknown.
