Story
Tagline
The UK's leading news, jobs, training and events for journalists.
Introduction
Journalism.co.uk reports on news and innovation in digital journalism, including the latest tools, apps and tips for journalists.

Founded in 1999, the site also offers a range of open and in-house training courses for journalists and media professionals, delivered by expert tutors.

Other services include a media jobs board and freelance directory, as well as PressGo press release distribution for PRs.

Journalism.co.uk also organises the popular news:rewired digital journalism conference, which is held at MSN UK in London.
