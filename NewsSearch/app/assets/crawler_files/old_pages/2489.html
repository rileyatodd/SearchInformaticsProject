Victoria

Melbourne

Australian Open Tennis. Perfect time to visit and catch the first Grand Slam of the year.
New South Wales

New England

Tamworth Country Music Festival. Get your dance on for the largest music festival in the southern hemisphere.
India

Jaipur

Jaipur Kite Festival 2015. The skies of Rajasthan are filled with colourful kites to mark the northward journey of the sun. It's considered to be an auspicious day when the kites are raised.
Northeast Asia

China

Harbin International Ice Festival. A wonderland of ice sculptures and laser-lights that will amaze.
Australia

Victoria

Festival of Sails. Head to Geelong this Australia Day weekend for yacht racing, live music and fireworks.
Italy

Venice

Venice Carnevale. Exquisite masks and costumes; it's dramatic and unforgettable experience.
Brazil

Rio de Janeiro

Rio Carnival. Cultural spectacular of music and dance with an extravagant parade of colourful floats.
Southeast Asia

Vietnam

Tet (Lunar New Year) 2015. Festivities start a week before - markets selling cumquat trees and houses adorned with peach blossoms. While most festivities involve quiet family celebrations, New Year's Eve gets raucous and rowdy with percussion everywhere.
Japan

Sapporo

Sapporo Snow Festival. Experience a wonderland of ice sculptures and snow statues at Odori Park.
Europe

Hungary

Budapest Fish Festival. The perfect spot to try Hungary's famous hot and spicy Fisherman's soup.
New South Wales

Sydney

Sydney Mardi Gras Parade. Provoking costumes and giant floats - it's the biggest of its kind in the word.
USA

Austin

SXSW Music Festival. Into music and multi-media? Don't miss this unique entertainment festival.
Spain

Valencia

Las Fallas March 2015. An annual pyrotechnics spectacular with dazzling visual displays of light and fire. Your eyes will be ringing with marching brass bands, live music, explosions and dancing.
Ireland

Dublin

St Patrick's Day. Parades, festivals and wearing green attire - that how's the Irish celebrate their patron saint.
India

Delhi

Holi Colour Festival. Dry powder, water balloons and a playful chase; join this spring festival celebration.
Northeast Asia

Japan

Cherry blossom season. Head to Yoshino, Japan's top cherry-blossom destination. It's a sight worth seeing.
Thailand

Chiang Mai

Songkran festival. Celebrate the Thai New Year in a mass street water celebration.
Europe

Turkey

ANZAC Day 2015. It is a once in a lifetime experience to camp out overnight at ANZAC Cove to see in the Dawn Service. This is the largest Anzac Day commemoration outside of Australia.
Southeast Asia

Philippines

Good Friday Crucifixions. In San Fernando, it's not just a dramatic retelling of the last moments of Jesus' life.
North America

USA

Coachella Music Festival 2015. Bask in two successive weekends of fabulous art and great music.
Europe

Monaco

Monaco Grand Prix 2015. Join Europe's wealthy and glamorous for some sea-side fun and fast-cars.
Czech Republic

Prague

Prague Spring International Music Festival. Experience the music that is symbolic of Central Europe.
Southeast Asia

Laos

Bun Bang Fai (Rocket Festival). Dance, theatre, processions and the launching of home-made rockets marks this Buddhist folk ceremony.
New South Wales

Country NSW

Australian Cotton Fibre Expo. See the finest cotton finished products in a town famous for its cotton fields.
France

Cannes

Cannes International Film Festival. For 12 days, this coastal city is transformed into a glitzy show of cinema.
Europe

Spain

El Colacho Festival 2015. Watch men, dressed as devils, jump over babies to cleanse their souls.
South America

Peru

Festival of the Sun. Traditionally, this winter solstice celebration would have involved mass murder.
England

London

Glastonbury Festival 2015. Well recognised for it's celebrity traction, this is England's biggest festival and arguably the best music festival in the world.

Morocco

Marrakesh

Ramadan. Once the sun sets, streets come alive with light displays, late-night feasts and nightly festivities.

Northeast Asia

Dragon Boat Festival. Celebrate the holiday with dumplings, wine and a colourful boat race.
North America

Canada

Calgary Stampede 2014. This is not the average rodeo festival, it's "The Greatest Outdoor Show On Earth".
France

Paris

Bastille Day. Parades, communal meals and fireworks commemorate this historical event.
Spain

Pamplona

San Fermin Festival 2014. The running of the bulls starts when the clock on the church of San Cernin strikes 8am. This adrenaline-rushing, one of a kind experience is part of San Fermin fiestas.
North Coast

Byron Bay

Splendour In The Grass 2014. Enjoy a weekend of music festivities in the crisp, cool Byron air.
Europe

Switzerland

Montreux International Jazz Festival 2014. It's an unmissable event for true music lovers.
Europe

Finland

Air Guitar World Championships 2014. This is not an event for music hobbyist.
Top End

Darwin

Darwin Aboriginal Art Fair 2014. A unique opportunity to see and buy artworks from Indigenous artists.
USA

Las Vegas

Burning Man Festival 2014. Once a year, Nevada's Black Rock Desert turns into an experiment in community, art and self-expression, and concludes with the burning of a large wooden effigy.
Scotland

Edinburgh

Edinburgh International Festival 2014. Enjoy three weeks of music and dance at the world's festival city.
Austria

Salzburg

Salzburg Festival. An epic festival of opera, drama and concerts hosted in the birthplace of Mozart.
Hungary

Budapest

Budapest International Wine Festival 2014. An ideal experience for anyone keen to learn about wine making.

ACT

Canberra

Floriade 2014. Dotted with over a million blooms, this floral festival is not-to-be-missed.
Europe

Germany

Oktoberfest 2015. Experience the best of Bavaria's culture, food and beer in this 16-day Munich festival which is a favourite among travellers. Put on a costume and get into the festive swing at one of the many colourful beer halls.
Europe

Spain

El Colacho Festival 2015. Watch men, dressed as devils, jump over babies to cleanse their souls.
Europe

England

Bestival 2014, Isle of Wight. Camp overnight for this four-day music festival.
South Asia

India

Diwali (Festival of Lights). Light a small clay lamp or candle to celebrate this ancient Hindu festival.
China

Macau

Macau International Music Festival. Be enchanted by opera, chamber music and Chinese folk music.
Spain

Barcelona

Les Festes de la Merce 2014. This is Barcelona's end-of-summer bash. The city comes alive with four days of street festivals - each day commemorated with its own parade of characters, dancing and music playing.

Spain

Ibiza

Ibiza Closing Parties 2014. Farewell the European summer on this party island.
Australia

New South Wales

Walcha Garden Festival 2014. See impressive garden displays on show.
Europe

Iceland

Iceland Airwaves 2014. Experience the musical edge of Nordic culture at this alternative rock festival.
North America

Mexico

Day of the Dead Festival. Be intrigued and fascinated at Mexico's carnival of the departed souls.
Europe

Austria

Krampusnacht 2015. This pre-Christmas carnival is not for the faint-hearted. If seeing Krampus, a demon-like creature said to derive from pagan origins, is more than spine tingling for you, hang around for the Feast of St. Nicholas in December to welcome some Christmas magic.
Germany

Berlin

The Berlin Wall. Along the wall, white balloons will be released to commemorate the end of the wall.
The Murray

Mildura

Mildura Jazz, Food and Wine Festival 2014. Sit back and enjoy five days of jazz, food, wine and paddle steamers.
Europe

Scotland

Hogmanay Street Festival. Start your New Year celebrations at the world-famous Street Party.
USA

New York City

New Years Eve. Welcome the new year watching the Times Square ball drop at midnight.
Australia

Tasmania

Falls Festival 2014-15. This music and arts festival is a relaxing alternative to the big festivals. Listen to acoustic artists, stand-up comedy, do a bit of line dancing or take part in some fun out-door activities.
Pacific

New Zealand

Rhythm and Vines Festival. Held at a vineyard, it's said to be the ultimate New Year festival experience.
Middle East

Israel

Christmas Eve. Join processions in Jerusalem and take the community walk to Bethlehem.
