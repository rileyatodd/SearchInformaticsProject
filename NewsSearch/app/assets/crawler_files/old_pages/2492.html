Storms no drought breaker for many

Click for more photos

Coonamble farmer Neil Kennedy on his "Kuma" station property mustering his cattle with his son Neil and station hand Scott White. The Kennedy's barley crop failed this year due to the drought that has gripped much of the North Western parts of the state. Fortunately for the Kennedys, they were able the fatten their cattle on the failed barley crop and recieve a good price for their cattle on the market. The cattle were sent to a Queensland abbatoir.
