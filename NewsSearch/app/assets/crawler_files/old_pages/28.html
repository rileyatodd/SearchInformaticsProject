    12.15.14

    Two Hostages Die in Sydney

    Sydney police say they killed a hostage-taker inside a café early Tuesday but that two hostages died after the 16-hour ordeal. Close to a dozen hostages had already fled the shop by the time police declared the situation under control. The victims were a 34-year-old man and a 38-year-old woman. Four others were injuried in the ordeal, inlcuding a police officer. The Lindt café was taken over by a gunman identified as Man Haron Monis, who was facing 50 charges of sexual assault and charged as an accessory to murder in his ex-wife’s death. (Monis was out on bail.) Australian police swarmed the café at around 2 a.m. local time. Gunfire and flashes were visible in footage from the scene, and police appeared to have stormed the café after a second group of hostages fled.
