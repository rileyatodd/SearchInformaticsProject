It’s freezing outside and you forgot to set your Nest Thermostat this morning — now you’re doomed to a house resembling the North Pole... No more! Fix that with a simple voice command: in the Google app on your Android or iPhone, just say “OK Google, set Nest to 73 degrees” and your house will be cozy and warm when you arrive. (Unless your significant other counters with a command to set the temp to their perfect 67 degrees!)

But if that’s too much work, you can get the perfect temperature at home with no hassle at all: your Google app can let your Nest know you’re on your way home and it’ll pre-heat or cool to your preferred temperature — and you’ll get a notification in return. Just go to workswithnest.google.com to set it up!﻿
