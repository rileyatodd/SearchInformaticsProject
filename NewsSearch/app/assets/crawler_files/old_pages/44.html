Pennsylvania police are searching for shooting suspect

Police in Pennsburg, Pennsylvania are searching for Bradley William Stone, an identified suspect in Monday's shooting spree, which left six people dead and one person wounded. Officials are advising Pennsburg residents to stay in their homes while the suspect is still at large.

The first shooting was reported shortly before 4:00 a.m. in Pennsylvania's Lower Salford Township. Additional shootings were reported in Lansdale and Souderton. All of the victims were related to Stone.

Police stated that Stone "should be considered armed and dangerous," ABC News reports. Officials advised residents to contact the police with any information about Stone's whereabouts.
