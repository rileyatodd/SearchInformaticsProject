Sierra Leone to start house-to-house searches for Ebola patients
Comments ( 0 )
The President of Sierra Leone, Ernest Bai Koroma says house-to-house search for Ebola patients will start today.

He said internal travel restrictions will also be imposed as part of a new push to combat the epidemic.

Rates of infection are rising in Sierra Leone, with more than half of 18,000 confirmed cases of the virus.
