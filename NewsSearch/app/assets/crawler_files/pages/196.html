Introduction
The Modern Ghana Homepage is Ghana’s leading general news and information destination online. Modernghana.com integrates credible Ghanaian content and perspectives.

Our name better reflects what this site is - and always has been - the Web site of A Modern Ghana. Since our establishment in August 2005, we have brought the news and information to all Ghanaians and to a large and growing online audience. 

Modern Ghana news is updated with increasing depth and frequency. Today, the site is updated 24 hours a day, seven days a week with follow-ups and breaking news, as well as original reporting, entertainment and sports. And our traffic has grown accordingly. 

Currently, the website enjoys traffic of over 80,000 unique visitors daily and many more when there is breaking news.
