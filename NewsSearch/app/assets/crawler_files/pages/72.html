Introduction
Welcome to the WLTX Google+ profile.

We're excited to use this platform to host discussions with you centered on the news of the day.

We'll share the best of what we do in breaking news, technology news, opinion, photos and video.

By using Google+, our goals are to:
  • Establish a community of people around the MIdlands, in Columbia, and those with connections to our area and state.
  • Engage in discussion with our community about news of the day.
  • Listen to our community and share news, photos and videos that will interest those who have circled us.
  • Take advantage of Google+'s unique platform to open our newsroom up to our readers. We'll hold video hangouts open to the public with reporters, editors and newsmakers.
We welcome your feedback! Let us know what you want to see from us on Google+. We can't wait to talk to you.
